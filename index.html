CONCEPT · SENSORISED SOCKET
P-01 ◉ ACTIVE · FLAGSHIP

UniSense

Dual-Mode Prosthetic Socket Mapping System

◇ PROBLEM

Prosthetic sockets are still fit by trial and error. Clinicians choose EMG control sites and manage pressure comfort separately — so a socket that controls well can still hurt, driving discomfort, tissue damage and device abandonment.

◈ SOLUTION

A wearable that simultaneously maps EMG control quality and pressure/temperature comfort across the residual limb, scores every candidate site and recommends locations that are both controllable and comfortable — backed by MATLAB analysis and COMSOL field modelling.

The integrated map fuses control-site separability with comfort hotspots into a single decision surface, so clinicians can pick electrode positions that maximise signal and wearability instead of trading one for the other.

EMGFSR PressureThermistorMATLABCOMSOLESP32

◆ Presented at the Australasian Bioelectronics, Neurosensing & Neuromodulation Symposium (ABNN) 2026.

P-02◉ PUBLISHED

Markerless Optical Myography

Non-contact bionic control · ~95% accuracy

◇ PROBLEM

EMG electrodes for prosthetic control need skin contact, gel and precise placement, and degrade with sweat and motion — limiting reliable multi-DOF control.

◈ SOLUTION

A camera-based, markerless optical myography method that reads muscle-surface deformation without contact, feeding an ML classifier that decodes multiple degrees of freedom at ~95% accuracy.

Computer VisionMachine LearningPythonSignal Processing

◆ Publication (2024): “Towards Markerless, Non-Contact Optical Myography for Prosthetic Control of Multiple Degrees-of-Freedom.” · IIT Delhi, RISE Lab.

P-05◉ PROTOTYPE · SIMULATION

Passive Clutch & Cable Routing

Posture-assist exo-mechanism for walking

◇ PROBLEM

Powered exoskeletons that assist gait and posture are heavy, costly and battery-limited, keeping mobility assistance out of reach for everyday rehab use.

◈ SOLUTION

A passive clutch + cable-routing mechanism (pelvis + thigh brace) that stores and releases energy at the right phase of the gait cycle to assist posture without motors — validated in OpenSim across loaded and unloaded walking.

The worn prototype pairs a pelvis brace clutch with a thigh brace and posterior spring. In gait testing, the suited knee-flexion profile tracks the baseline closely while redistributing load — evidence the passive assist works without disrupting natural walking.

◆ Inverse kinematics + CMC / static optimization across load conditions.

P-06◉ FEM · COMSOL

Retinal Implant Field Modelling

Selective electrode stimulation in COMSOL Multiphysics

◇ PROBLEM

Retinal prostheses must excite target neurons precisely. Poor electrode configuration spreads current, blurring artificial vision and risking tissue damage.

◈ SOLUTION

A COMSOL FEM comparing monopolar, bipolar, tripolar and hexapolar arrays across electrode radii — quantifying field confinement, current density and contrast to select the sharpest, safest, most selective configuration.

Contrast analysis across retinal layers showed the hexapolar configuration gives the tightest field focus — trading a little leakage for markedly higher spatial selectivity, the key to high-resolution prosthetic vision.

P-07◉ RESEARCH · STANDARDS

Bioinformatics-Guided Platelet Assay

Modernising ISO 10993-4 hemocompatibility testing

◇ PROBLEM

Standard ISO 10993-4 platelet-activation testing for blood-contacting devices suffers from donor variability, static (non-flow) conditions and poor cross-lab standardisation — making hemocompatibility results hard to reproduce.

◈ SOLUTION

A three-layer dynamic platelet activation assay: microfluidic flow chambers, iPSC-derived reference platelets (low + high-reactivity anchors), and a computational layer that normalises every run into one unitless Platelet Activation Risk Score.

By anchoring each run to reproducible cell-derived controls and modelling donor, batch, chip and operator effects explicitly, the assay attacks all three failure modes at once — as a standardisation layer over conventional testing.

◆ Evidence-backed modernization pathway for hemocompatibility standardization.
